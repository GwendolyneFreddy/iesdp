--- n: 66 name: "Graphics: Transparency Fade" param1: "Fade Amount" param2: "Visual Effect" bg1: 1 bg2: 1 bgee: 1 iwd1: 0 iwd2: 0 pst: 0 --- Fades the targeted creature(s) by the value in the amount specified by the Fade Amount field in the style specified by the Visual Effect field.
Known values for Fade Amount are:
  • 1 ⟶ Fully visible
  • ...
  • 255 ⟶ Almost invisible

Known values for Visual Effect are:
  • 0 ⟶ Draw instantly
  • 1 ⟶ Fade in
  • 2 ⟶ Fade out


This effect sets TRANSLUCENT to Fade Amount.
