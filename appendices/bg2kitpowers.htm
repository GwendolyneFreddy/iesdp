BG2: ToB Kit Specific Settings

Creatures in BG2 can have a specific kit, stored at 0x0244 in the CRE file. Several of these kits are hard-coded to have specific restrictions (e.g. lowered thief skill progression); these restrictions are listed below.

Swashbuckler (0x00100000)
Do not follow the standard backstab bonus progression scheme

Bounty Hunter (0x00080000)
Receive only 20 thief skill points per level

Assassin (0x00040000)
Receive only 15 thief skill points per level
